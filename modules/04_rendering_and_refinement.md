## Module name
04_rendering_and_refinement

## Purpose
Combine intermediate data into final outputs under required headings.

## Inputs
- section_summaries

- terminology_map

- target_audience

## Outputs

2. Build Section-by-Section Summary.
3. Generate Expert Summary.

5. Insert Mini-Glossary.
6. Append Checks & Warnings.
7. Consistency pass.
8. Output final_markdown.
